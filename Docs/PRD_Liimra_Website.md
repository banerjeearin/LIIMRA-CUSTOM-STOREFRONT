## 11. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

### Q1: Is Liimra flour really freshly ground?
**Answer:** Yes — we grind every batch within 48 hours of your order. We don't stockpile flour in warehouses. Each bag is sealed immediately after grinding to lock in freshness and aroma.

### Q2: Is it safe for diabetics?
**Answer:** Ragi and Jowar flour both have a low glycaemic index and are widely recommended by dietitians for diabetics. They slow glucose absorption and reduce post-meal sugar spikes. As always, consult your doctor for personalised dietary advice.

### Q3: What is the shelf life?
**Answer:** Because we don't use preservatives, we recommend consuming within 60 days of purchase. Store in a cool, dry place or refrigerate to extend freshness further.

### Q4: Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
**Answer:** Yes, COD is available pan-India. You pay only when your order is delivered to your doorstep. We also accept UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking.

### Q5: What if I'm not satisfied?
**Answer:** We offer a 30-day, no-questions-asked refund policy. If you're not happy with your order for any reason, WhatsApp us and we'll arrange a full refund or replacement immediately.

### Q6: Can children eat these flours?
**Answer:** Absolutely — Ragi especially is recommended for children and infants. Its high calcium content supports bone and teeth development. Ragi porridge (kanji) has been a traditional weaning food in South India for generations.
